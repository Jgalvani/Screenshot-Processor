Notes:
------
- The tool expects absolute URLs (starting with http:// or https://)
- URLs can be placed as plain text or as hyperlinks in the Word document
- URLs in tables are also supported
- Duplicate URLs will be automatically filtered out
- Full page screenshots are now enabled by default (SCREENSHOT_FULL_PAGE=true)
- Sites with products requiring size/color selection may not show prices until options are chosen
- Major retailers (Amazon, Walmart, Target, Best Buy) often block automated browsers
